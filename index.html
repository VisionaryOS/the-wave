Breakthrough 28.01.26

GPT-5 doesn't just understand you. It reasons with you.

OpenAI just shipped something that feels different. Not incrementally better—fundamentally different. GPT-5 doesn't just predict the next word. It builds mental models.

The demos are unsettling in the best way. Feed it a physics problem and watch it sketch diagrams, backtrack when it hits dead ends, and explain its reasoning like a patient tutor. This isn't autocomplete with better training data. This is something new.

The implications ripple outward: scientific research accelerates, education transforms, and the definition of "intelligence" gets hazier. The question isn't whether AI will change everything. It's whether we're ready to change with it.
